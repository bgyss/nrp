## Delivered Work and Measured Results

- **Scale, streaming, and quality gates (T1–T4).** Added 512² real-scene
  ingestion, packed-cache streamed training, perceptual approval gates, and
  the WebGPU export/runtime path. The committed T4 report records PyTorch
  parity of `1.22e-6` max absolute difference and 512² Chrome p95 latency of
  23.1 ms (43.3 fps p95), meeting its 30 fps p95 criterion.
- **Games (G1/G2).** Added invalidated-shard residual compositing and a
  browser demo with scripted interaction/gating. The existing close-out report
  records G1 as an honest partial: 37.15 dB against a 44.88 dB recovery
  reference, while out-of-mask fidelity is 54.90 dB. G2 recorded 481
  interactions at 512² with 30.7 ms p95 and 12/12 sampled frames passing its
  preview gate.
- **Film (F1/F2).** Added temporal FLIP-delta checks, a 120-frame shot
  harness, final-tier residual-identity frames, and an MP4 artifact. F1
  records 120/120 preview passes and worst temporal excess of 0.0019; F2
  records 120/120 final passes and 118x wall-clock amortization. F2 remains a
  storage negative: proxy plus residuals are 1.17x raw-frame storage.
- **VFX (V1/V2).** Added per-light models, solo/mute compositing, color
  reparameterization, target recovery, and a headless slider loop. V1's
  preview additivity gate passes PSNR (25.61 dB) but fails SSIM (0.6225) and
  FLIP (0.3521); it is documented as a negative, not a success. V2's
  image-space recovery passes its draft gate (154.54 dB), reloads identically,
  but takes 950.1 ms mean per slider adjustment and only sphere-light colors
  were genuinely recoverable before the QuadLight fix.
- **Hardening H1.** The QuadLight failure was narrowed from a presumed training
  budget issue to a quad/type correlation on the kitchen cache. The fix,
  documentation, and test coverage landed in `e738721`; V1/V2 conclusions
  remain caveated until the corrected rig is retrained and re-gated.
